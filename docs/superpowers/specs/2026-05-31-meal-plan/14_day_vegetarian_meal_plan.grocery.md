# 14-Day Meal Plan — Weekly Grocery List

Derived from `14_day_vegetarian_meal_plan.improved.md`. Week 1 = Days 1-7, Week 2 = Days 8-14. Quantities are summed by counting occurrences in the plan; round up at the store. The plan repeats the same skeleton (whey + milk + dal + grain + sabzi + salad + nut/seed), so pantry staples don't change week to week.

> **How to read:** `Item — W1 qty / W2 qty — notes`

## Notes & Sourcing (Charlotte NC)

- **Harris Teeter / Publix / Food Lion / Costco** — produce, dairy, tofu, tempeh, oats, quinoa, brown rice, base nuts/seeds, specialty nuts (Costco bulk is cheapest for almonds/walnuts/pecans).
- **Patel Brothers / India Grocers** — all dals, bajra/jowar/ragi flours, besan, paneer (fresher than chain stores), soya chunks, makhana, poha, idli/dosa batter, sambar powder, chironji, hing.
- **Online / health food store** — tiger nuts, algal omega-3.

## What you do NOT need to buy every week

- Pantry spices, oil, ghee, salt (monthly restock at most)
- Whey (1 tub lasts ~6 weeks at 1 scoop/day)
- Specialty nuts (100-200g bag = 1 month supply)
- Supplements (monthly)
- Dry dals/grains — buy in 500g-1kg packs; one pack covers 2-3 cycles of the 14-day plan
